Verification ladder

Verification is useful only when it says what kind of proof it is. These stages prevent verifier green lights from becoming fake income proof.

  1. Source validity: official URL, date/status, access limitation, and kill rule.
  2. Model usefulness: useful, risky, or too generic feedback with a stated blocker.
  3. Repeatability: the same lane works across three official sources and rejects stale rows.
  4. Income proof: only $100+ third-party paid, funded, settled, escrowed, or claimable evidence.
